Welcome to Jackson Heights

The most diversed Neighborhood in New York City

Jackson Heights is one of the most culturally diversed Neighborhood in New York. It's been home to thousands of immigrants all over the world. With these cultural diversity, Jackson Heights provides unique experience for everyone living there. Also these immigrants has opened many small businesses show casing their different cultural side which makes Jackson Heights colorful.
